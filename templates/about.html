About Book2Movie

Book2Movies is a platform that provides movie recommendations based on your favorite books. We aim to bridge the gap between book lovers and movie enthusiasts by offering a curated list of movies that are either adaptations of popular books or have similar themes and stories. Our goal is to enhance your entertainment experience by connecting you to the best of both worlds.

Our recommendation system is designed to analyze the content of books and movies to identify similarities and This uses cosine similarity to analyze book and movie texts, ensuring accurate recommendations based on themes, characters, and narratives. Book2Movie is a recommendation system project that leverages data from Goodreads and movie databases to provide book-to-movie recommendations. The project includes a web application, data analysis scripts, and a comprehensive report on the methodology used.
